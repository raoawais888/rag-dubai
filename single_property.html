About This Project

Lamaa is a newly launched project with premium apartments in one of Dubai’s most vibrant neighborhoods, Madinat Jumeirah Living (MJL). Properties in the complex include stunning apartments next to the iconic Burj Al Arab with classic Arabic architecture, which is surrounded by lush green grounds.

Properties are distinguished by open-plan living areas and huge terraces facing towards the inner courtyards. Residents of the Madinat Jumeirah Living Lamaa complex will be able to enjoy stunning views of the Burj Al Arab, Jumeirah Beach Hotel, Marsa Al Arab, as well as the skyline of the Jumeirah area.
