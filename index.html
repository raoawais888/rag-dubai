About PSI

Established in 2007, Property Shop Investment (PSI) is one of the leading real estate firms serving the entire UAE landscape. Driven by passion for success, PSI has been awarded number 1 real estate agent by key giant developers for the past years. PSI continues to innovate and increase its services to ensure seamless and comprehensive clients journeys.

Why PSI

Our solid reputation in the industry is supported by our continuous pursuit of quality and superiority in all the services we offer. Our combination of vision, expertise, specialised teams, enthusiasm and transparency entitles Property Shop Investment to set the market standard as an influencer of Abu Dhabi's Real Estate Market

As an award-winning, full-service brokerage, Property Shop Investment is known for exceeding all expectations. We aim for the highest performance and have been awarded the 'Number One Real Estate Broker in Abu Dhabi' for then consecutive years.

PSI's emphasis on delivering straightforward and reliable services to corporate and private clients ensures complete customer satisfaction. All services are tailor-made to meet customer demands and our commitment transforms a promise into reality

Our Heroes

Committed and self motivated professionals who are driven by passion for success. Our highly qualified professionals have an in-depth knowledge of economics, marketing, real estate, and customer service. We ensure that they all have the essential tools to enhance and develop their skills.

The company culture is unique, and every employee is proud to wear the PSI badge and to be part of its distinct environment. While each member of our multi-cultural team embraces their personal achievements, all members believe in supporting one another to achieve the company vision and celebrate collective success.

Our Sevices

SALES

The sales team is the advisor of buyers; they coordinate with the sales listing team and act as an intermediary between the buyer and seller. This process helps clients navigate the complex nature of the property market; the agents intermediate the negotiation process, consult clients on market conditions, prices, mortgages, legal requirements and all required documentation to ensure securing a fair and honest deal. Our sales team offers the best advice to clients to suit their financial position and protect their interests.

LANDLORDS

The listing department is divided into two teams, a sales listing team and a leasing listing team. The listing agents are the advisors of sellers and landlords; they make sure to maximize the value of their clients' properties and also list them with the heighest level of confidentiality. The listing department is heavily influenced by the marketing team's work, and therefore they always communicate to ensure promoting of the listing teamn resulted in the highest inventory of property listings in UAE

LEASING

The leasing team is the advisor of tenants; they coordinate with the leasing listing team and act as an intermediary between the tenant and landlord. The leasing agents determine the needs, standards and economic viability of their customers, provide them with information on all the aspects of properties and negotiate leasing terms on behalf of the potential tenant

CRM

Our teams are supported with an inhouse developed PSI engine and CRM system to enable seamless operations for agents and business functions

Data is protected under our privacy policy to ensure the confidentiality of information and protect the privacy of clients.

MARKETING

PSI has built its own marketing and creative hub with specialties in the different offline and online fields that serve milestones of our clients' journeys.

The Multi-Channeled marketing efforts driven by our team's experiences ensure an indepth understanding of stakeholders behaviors and what they are looking for.

Marketing teams support developers, investors, sellers and landlord by creating an identity and a seamless journey while building a solid brand loyalty and recognition.

Furthermore, the marketing department has a team specialized in advertising properties in more than fifty portals and company websites.
